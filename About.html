What is this website for?

Welcome to our dedicated platform for submitting applications related to the application for new / replacement of identification card at Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP). Our website is designed to provide a straightforward and efficient experience for students seeking to apply for a new identification card or request a replacement.
